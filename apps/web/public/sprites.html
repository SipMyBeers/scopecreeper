Behavior plan: when a thread is focused and result.dimensions[] has at least 1 entry, the five hotspots become buttons. Each one is given an aria-label of the dimension's label, a tooltip showing the blurb, and an onClick that calls creep.drillInto(dimension). Cables not bound to a dimension stay dim; bound cables pulse the cable's neon color. After the drill, the readout updates and the cable's hotspot now represents the next-level dimensions (recursive CYOA).
